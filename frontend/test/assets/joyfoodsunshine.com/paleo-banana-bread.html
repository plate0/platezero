Paleo Banana Bread

Last updated on March 23, 2019. Originally published January 4, 2018 56 Comments

Hands down, this is the best Paleo Banana Bread recipe you will ever try! It’s easy to make, super healthy & crazy delicious! 

There’s something about the cold weather that makes me seriously crave banana bread! Really, when are bananas not in season? They’re always available at the grocery store (or gas station or drug store) for a reasonable price, I always have some on hand in the freezer, and I’m never without a few overripe bananas sitting on my counter.

However it’s January and lots of you lovely ladies and gents are thinking about living a healthier lifestyle. And I want you to know {like really know} that healthy living & eating does NOT mean sacrificing your favorite foods. Many classic treats can be made healthier, like this Paleo Banana Bread! This version is gluten & grain-free, dairy-free and made with zero refined sugar, but is just as irresistibly delicious as it’s sugar/butter laden counterparts.

And I have to say, that this is the very best grain-free banana bread I have ever eaten! Here are a few reasons why I love this recipe, and you will too!

  • It’s so moist and dense, yet has a beautiful crumb!
  • The ingredient list is short, and consists of things you probably always have on hand!
  • It delivers a nutritional punch and is actually good for you!

Seriously people, I’m saying goodbye to all my other banana bread recipes and will only be making this Paleo Banana Bread from now until the end of time.

How to make Paleo Banana Bread

This healthy banana bread recipe is super simple to make! I love that all the ingredients can be mixed in one bowl, and that it only takes 5 minutes of prep time to make this recipe! Here are a few step-by-step photos showing how to make this paleo banana bread (don’t forget ot watch the video too)!

My daughter Bethany asks for this bread for dessert! Yes folks, she chooses this over brownies and ice cream and popsicles! {Oh my}! The only other time she chooses a healthy dessert over a “regular” one is when I whip up these Chocolate Peanut Butter Coconut Bars!

I love that I can serve a dessert (or snack or breakfast) that is gluten-free, dairy-free, paleo and sweetened with a measly 2 TBS of maple syrup! Yep, total mom win…if I decide to share…because come on this stuff is so good. 🙅🏻 🍌 🍞

Paleo Banana Bread: Substitutions 

Here are a few potential substitutions that can be made in this recipe. I do not recommend altering the amount or type of flour at all. Paleo baking is truly an art, and the ratios & kinds of flours used in this recipe were carefully tested and chosen!

  • Coconut oil. Grass-fed butter or ghee can be used in place of coconut oil!
  • Almond butter. I have also made this recipe with cashew butter with super delicious results! You can try it with any nut or seed butter, but I cannot vouch for the outcome {if you do, leave a comment and let me know how it went}!
  • Maple Syrup. Honey is a totally acceptable and delicious substitution for maple syrup!
  • Chocolate chips. Are optional, but not really. Use Enjoy Life Foods or Sunspire chocolate chips for allergy friendly options!

Paleo Banana Bread

Hands down, this is the best Paleo Banana Bread recipe you will ever try! It's easy to make, super healthy & crazy delicious! 

Course bread, Breakfast
Cuisine American
Keyword gluten-free banana bread recipe, healthy banana bread, healthy banana bread recipe, paleo banana bread
Prep Time 10 minutes
Cook Time 30 minutes
Total Time 40 minutes
Servings 10 slices
Calories 205.1 kcal
Author Laura

Ingredients

  • 2 large overripe bananas about 1 cup, mashed
  • ¼ cup coconut oil
  • ¼ cup creamy almond butter
  • 2 TBS pure maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup of coconut flour
  • ¼ cup almond flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp sea salt
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line an 8x4” baking pan (you can use a 9x5” pan but your loaf will be thinner and baking time will be less) with parchment paper and grease. Set aside.
  2. In a small bowl combine coconut flour, almond flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon). Set aside.
  3. In a large, glass mixing bowl melt together coconut oil and almond butter (for about 1 minute on high), stir until combined.
  4. Add mashed banana, maple syrup and vanilla extract and mix.
  5. Add eggs and stir until smooth.
  6. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until completely combined.
  7. Gently mix in chocolate chips.
  8. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for 30-40 minutes, until top is set and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  9. Let cool in the pan for 5 minutes before gently removing the loaf from the pan to a wire rack to cool.
  10. Enjoy!
